I looked to the Nature Conservancy and Ocean Conservancy as inspiration for my enviormental website. I was able to identify key features my potential audience might want, such as a section for news articles and other resources.

My main goal at the start of the design process was to create a central networking hub for the marine community, so I looked to scientific networking sites such as reasearchgate.com to gain a better understanding of how I could integrate a social network into my web app.
